Introduction

Welcome to StudyFlow. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

StudyFlow is a student productivity app designed to help you focus, manage notes, track your CGPA, set goals, and prepare for exams.

🛡️

Data We Do NOT Collect

StudyFlow does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • Financial or payment information
  • Any data transmitted to external servers
💾

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you create in StudyFlow is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. This includes:

  • Notes and their content
  • CGPA and subject grades
  • Daily goals and progress
  • Exam dates and reminders
  • Pomodoro study session history
  • Timer preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

🔐

App Permissions

StudyFlow requests only the permissions necessary for core features:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — To send exam reminders and study goal alerts
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — To deliver exam countdown notifications at the right time
  • VIBRATE — To provide haptic feedback during timer completion
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — To reschedule exam reminders after device restart

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit any data.

🔗

Third Party Services

StudyFlow does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. There are no SDKs that collect your data in the background.

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or any similar services.

☁️

Google Backup

StudyFlow supports Android's built-in backup system. If you have Google backup enabled on your device, your app data (notes, goals, CGPA records) may be backed up to your personal Google account.

This backup is managed entirely by Google and your personal Google account settings. We have no access to this data. You can disable this in your device's backup settings at any time.

👦

Children's Privacy

StudyFlow is suitable for users of all ages including children and students. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk to children's privacy.

Our app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Since we do not collect your contact information, we recommend checking this page periodically for any updates.
